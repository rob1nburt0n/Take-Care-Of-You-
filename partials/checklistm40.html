Checklist Male Ages 40-49

Physical (every 1-3 years, depending on risk factors)

Dermatology

Eye Exam

Prostate and/or Colorectal screening if high risk

Body Mass Index (BMI)

Cholesterol screening (at least every 5 years)

Diabetes screening (if high risk or every three years beginning at age 45)

Blood Pressure evaluated

Evaluate risk of STDs

Evaluate risk of Tuberulosis (TB)

Evaluate risk of Hep B and/or Hep C

HIV screening (annually for high risk)
