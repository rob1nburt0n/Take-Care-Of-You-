Checklist Female Ages 50-59

Annual Physical

Dermatology

Eye Exam once every 1-3 years at physician discretion

Monthly breast self-exam

Yearly clinical breast exam and yearly mammography

Pap test every three years or Pap test and HPV test every five years

Discuss management of menopause if needed

Colonoscopy at age 50 and then every 10 years

Body Mass Index (BMI)

Cholesterol screening every five years

Diabetes screening every three years

Blood Pressure evaluated

Evaluate risk of STDs

Evaluate risk of Tuberulosis (TB)

Evaluate risk of Hep B and/or Hep C

HIV screening (annually for high risk)
